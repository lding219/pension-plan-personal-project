# The Influence of Shocks to the US Public Pension Plans
The project is going to answer the following research question: How do macroeconomic shocks and public health shocks differentially affect the funding position of U.S. public pension plans, and through which actuarial channels (assets, liabilities, and contributions) do these effects operate?
### Data
The data used for this report is from the new Public Plans Database (PPD) constructed by the Center for Retirement Research at Boston College, the U.S. Census Bureau, the NCSL, and the Current Population Survey (CPS).
